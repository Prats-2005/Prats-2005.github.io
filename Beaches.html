Dive into the beaches of North Goa!
Calangute Beach

Dubbed the Queen of Beaches, Calangute Beach is unarguably one of the most popular beaches in Goa. It is also the busiest beach in North Goa with restaurants, shacks, and clubs fringing it.

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is where you will find water sports, fine dining restaurants, bars, and clubs. Situated in North Goa, Baga Beach is bordered by Calangute and Anjuna Beaches.

Candolim Beach

One of the longest beaches in Goa, Candolim Beach is also sometimes called the gateway to the other popular beaches in the state.

Anjuna Beach

Amongst the most popular beaches in Goa, Anjuna Beach, that stretches almost 2 km is popular amongst families and water sports lovers and one of the best hubs in Goa for nightlife.

Arambol Beach

One of the most beautiful beaches in North Goa, Arambol or Harmal Beach is a must-see on your holiday in the state; a silvery sand beach that also has rocks making it look quite stunning.

Sinquerim Beach

Sinquerim Beach is located just beside Aguada Fort, a major tourist attraction in Goa with immense historical significance. So, there’s no reason why you should visit the latter & miss out on the former.

Dive into the beaches of South Goa!
Palolem Beach

The most popular beach of South Goa, enclosed by a thick forest of coconut palms, Palolem is also one of the most picturesque beaches in the state.

Varca Beach

The appeal of Varca Beach lies in the fact that it is relatively uncrowded compared to the other beaches in Goa, and it's a great location too, as it’s situated just 45 minutes from the airport.

Baina Beach

Baina Beach is known for the suitable conditions and facilities that it offers, aiding a wide array of water sports and it is also popular for cultural programs.

Majorda Beach

Majorda Beach is one of the prettiest beaches of Goa. Soft golden sand, coconut trees swaying to the gentle wind & the sea breeze gently brushing your skin, make you fall in love with it.

Cavelossim Beach

Want to watch dolphins? Come to Cavelossim Beach, a beautiful strip of sand. A bit crowded compared to the other beaches of Goa, it provides you with plenty of options for having a wonderful time.

Betalbatim Beach

Neatly located between Colva Beach and Majorda Beach in Goa, Betalbatim Beach is a pristine place with golden sand spread all across the coastline, offering beautiful sunrise and sunset views.
